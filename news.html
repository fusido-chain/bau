INTRODUCTION

The disruption of technology in musical industry nowadays has changed the paradigm of the industry itself in many ways. It has changed how people run the industry into new model of business. It begins when the digitalization rules the musical industry on the past decade. We take this as golden chance to integrate blockchain technology and music industry, especially dangdut.

Dangdut Verse (DVerse) is the one and only decentralized Dangdut Music Ecosystem built on BEP-20 network supported by Dangdut Lovers and Dangdut Community in Indonesia. We apply blockchain technology on dangdut music ecosystem that will benefit for both variable and create the sustainability of the ecosystem.

VISION & MISSION

We trully believe that community is most important matter in dangdut ecosystem. Dverse will push dangdut community to stimulate the creativity from dangdut community to create and enjoy dangdut culture that connected with blockchain technology, Defi and Web 3.

Thus we can conquer the world with community power that support each other. Rising awareness in building dangdut ecosystem in digital world based on blockchain technology is one of main goals.
